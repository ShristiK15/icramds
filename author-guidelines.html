Author Guidelines

Paper Submission

Papers for submission (Maximum 10 pages only) need to be prepared in the Springer format using the templates available online.

Authors need to register themselves in the conference and present their papers in hybrid mode (Limited papers will be allowed for online presentations).

Selected papers presented at ICRAMDS-2025 will be submitted to Springer for possible publication in Springer Proceedings (to be confirmed) after a standard peer-review process.

Preamble and Objectives

  • Internationally recognized keynote speakers
  • Workshops and hands-on sessions
  • Paper presentations across multiple tracks
  • Networking opportunities with academia and industry
  • Panel discussions on emerging technologies
  • Publication in reputed journals and proceedings

Full-length Paper Submission

Prospective author(s) are invited to submit the full-length paper in the given format electronically by 31 August 2025. The length of the paper shall be limited to 10 pages as per the format. One of the authors must register and present the paper for inclusion in the conference proceedings. Selected papers will be published in Scopus-indexed Springer conference proceedings after peer review. Esteemed authors are requested to visit the conference website for further details. [Link] MANIT.

Important note

Selected papers from the conference will be published by Springer as a proceedings book volume (LNCS). Springer will conduct quality checks on the accepted papers, and only papers that pass these checks will be published. Springer Nature does not charge any money for publication of Non-Open Access content. Abstracts/extended abstracts and short papers (less than 10 pages) are not considered for publication.

*Only manuscripts below 20% similarity index (By plagiarism check) will be considered for review and further processing.
