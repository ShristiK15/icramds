Call for Papers

Submission Invitation

The International Conference on Recent Advances in Mathematics and Data Science (ICRAMDS-2025) invites researchers, scientists, students, and industry experts to submit original research contributions. This conference provides a vital platform for sharing innovative ideas and groundbreaking findings in the interconnected fields of mathematics and data science.We encourage submissions exploring applied mathematics, statistical modeling, machine learning, artificial intelligence, data analytics, and other relevant areas. Join us in advancing knowledge and shaping the future of these rapidly evolving fields.

Preamble and Objectives

Technology significantly impacts human existence, introducing both risks and enhancements to our lives. As society strives to create and adopt more efficient methods for living and extending life expectancy, the potential threats associated with technological advancement continue to escalate. The rapid progression of technology, particularly in the 20th and 21st centuries, has brought forth unparalleled benefits. The primary objective of the upcoming International Conference “ICRAMDS-2025” is to establish a forum for researchers, engineers, academicians, and industry professionals. This platform will facilitate the presentation and exploration of the integral role of Mathematics and Data Science in fostering a sustainable future, alongside recent advancements, trends, and developments occurring worldwide.

Full-length Paper Submission

Prospective author(s) are invited to submit the full-length paper in the given format electronically by 31 August 2025. The length of the paper shall be limited to 10 pages as per the format. One of the authors must register and present the paper for inclusion in the conference proceedings. Selected papers will be published in Scopus-indexed Springer conference proceedings after peer review. Esteemed authors are requested to visit the conference website for further details. [Link] MANIT.

Important note

Selected papers from the conference will be published by Springer as a proceedings book volume (LNCS). Springer will conduct quality checks on the accepted papers, and only papers that pass these checks will be published. Springer Nature does not charge any money for publication of Non-Open Access content. Abstracts/extended abstracts and short papers (less than 10 pages) are not considered for publication.

*Only manuscripts below 20% similarity index (By plagiarism check) will be considered for review and further processing.
